Wiki Loves Onam

Wiki Loves Onam is a photography campaign in Wikimedia Commons encourages participants to capture high-quality images of various aspects of Onam celebrations in different regions on categories, such as, Onam festivals, activities and traditions related to Onam, dance and games, Onam dishes, rituals and ceremonies, flowers etc.
